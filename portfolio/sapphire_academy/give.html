Giving

At Sapphire Academy, we encourage members to contribute to the growth and development of the community. Your contributions - whether volunteer hours, financial aid, gifts in kind - help provide an innovative programme for students; ensure a diverse student body through financial aid; help teach the students the importance of assisting the community for all families and alumni.

Save the date of our Annual Fund Raiser slated for Monday, November 28 2022.

The gifts we receive at Sapphire Academy willbe used to support the following ventures:

  • Innovative and Inclusive curriculum that inspires passion for learning
  • Financial aid to support families
  • Scholarships for students
  • Art programmes that make learning more engaging
  • Atheletic programmes that empower students with leadership skills that they can apply on and off the field.
