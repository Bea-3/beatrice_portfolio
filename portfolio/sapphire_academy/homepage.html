Why Apply to Sapphire Academy

With a focus on the whole child, we promote and foster individual passions, talents and aspirations.

We focus on educational innovation and challenging traditional paradigms through research and innovative program. And, we encourage learning from eachother.

We foster connections with our students within and outside the classroom and encourage them to exceed their limits

Our facilities provide opportunities for developmentally appropriate learning experiences.

Sapphire Academy

At Sapphire Academy, We focus on building self-knowledge, confidence, understanding and awareness at the Early years of a child's life. The primary school education consists of learning that is personalized to their individual interests as they draw connections between the classroom and the world around them. In their final years, secondary schoolers are encouraged to pursue their passions while developing their leadership skills.
